Report an Animal in Need

Your quick action can save a life

If you've spotted a stray, injured, or abandoned animal, please fill out this form.

Emergency Situations

If the animal is severely injured, in immediate danger, or posing a threat, please call our emergency hotline immediately:

317-738-3911

What Information to Include

  • Detailed location (address, landmarks, GPS)
  • Physical description of the animal
  • Animal's condition and behavior
  • Photos if possible (but don't risk your safety)
  • Your contact information for follow-up

What Happens Next

  1. Our team reviews your report (usually within 1-2 hours)
  2. A rescue coordinator may contact you for more details
  3. We dispatch a rescue team based on urgency
  4. The animal is brought to our shelter for assessment
  5. You'll receive an update on the animal's status

Thank you for being their voice! Your compassion saves lives.

Safety Tips When Approaching Stray Animals

  • 1. Keep Your Distance: Approach slowly and don't corner them. Stay calm and speak softly.
  • 2. Avoid Direct Eye Contact: It can be perceived as threatening to scared animals.
  • 3. Offer Food Carefully: Place it down and step back rather than hand-feeding.
  • 4. Never Force Interaction: If the animal seems aggressive or frightened, keep your distance and call us.
