Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

To adopt a pet, browse our available animals, fill out the adoption application, and schedule a meet-and-greet. Our team will guide you through the next steps.

Adoption fees typically range from Rs.50 to Rs.200 and include vaccinations, microchipping, and spay/neuter surgery.

Visit our Volunteer page, complete the interest form, and attend an orientation. Volunteers help with animal care, events, and more.

We offer a return policy. If things don’t work out, we encourage you to contact us. We'll work with you to find a better match or take the pet back.

Contact our surrender team at intake@pawpal.org. We’ll assess the situation and guide you through the process.
