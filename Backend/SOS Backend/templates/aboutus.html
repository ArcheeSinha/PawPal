About PawPal

Connecting pets to their dream home

Our Mission

At PawPal, we believe every pet deserves a loving home. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned and surrendered pets, while educating the community about responsible pet ownership.

Founded in 2013, we've helped over 5,000 pets find their forever homes. Our dedicated team of staff and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure each animal receives the care, attention, and love they deserve while they wait for their perfect match.
